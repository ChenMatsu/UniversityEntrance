### Derivative of Trigonometric

$$
    \begin{align*}
        \dfrac{d}{dx}\sin(x) & = \cos(x) \\
    \end{align*}
$$

$$
    \begin{align*}
        \dfrac{d}{dx}\tan(x) & = \dfrac{d}{dx}\dfrac{\sin(x)}{\cos(x)} = \dfrac{\cos^2(x) + \sin^2(x)}{\cos^2(x)} = \dfrac{1}{\cos^2(x)} = \sec^2(x)\\
    \end{align*}
$$
