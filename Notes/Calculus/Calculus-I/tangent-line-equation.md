### Tangent Line Equation

$$
    m = \dfrac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \dfrac{y - y_0}{x - x_0}
$$

$$
    y - y_0 = m(x - x_0) \\ 
    y - f(x_0) = f'(x_0)(x - x_0)
$$

**Example**
Find the equation of the tangent line to $f(x) = x^2 - 1$ at $x = 1$

$$
    f'(x) = 2x, f'(1) = 2 = m
$$

$$
    y = 2(x - 1)
$$
