### Derivative Rule

**Additivity**

$$
    \dfrac{d}{dx}(f(x) + g(x)) = \dfrac{d}{dx}f(x) + \dfrac{d}{dx}g(x)
$$

**Scalar Multiplication**

$$
    \dfrac{d}{dx}(cf(x)) = c\dfrac{d}{dx}f(x)
$$

**Power Rule**

$$
    \dfrac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}
$$

**Product Rule**

$$
    (fg)' = f'g + fg'
$$

**Quotient Rule**

$$
    (\dfrac{f}{g})' = \dfrac{f'g - fg'}{g^2}
$$
