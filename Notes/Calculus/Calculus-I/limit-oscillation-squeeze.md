### Limits of Oscillation and Squeeze Theorem

$$
    \lim_{x\to0^+}\sin(\dfrac{1}{x}) = \text{D.N.E}
$$
> Oscillating around 0

$$
    \lim_{x\to0^+}x\sin(\dfrac{1}{x}) = 0
$$
> Envelop Function $h(x) = x, f(x) = -x$

**Squeeze Theorem**

$$
    f(x) \le g(x) \le h(x) \\
    \lim_{x\to a}f(x) = \lim_{x\to a}h(x) = L \\
    \lim_{x\to a}g(x) = L
$$
