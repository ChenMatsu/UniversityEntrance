### Gabriel's Horn

- Surface Area: $\int_a^b2πf(x)\sqrt{1 + f'(x)^2}dx$
- Volume: $\int_a^bπf(x)^2dx$

$$
    f(x) = \dfrac{1}{x}
$$

$$
    SA = \int_1^∞2π\dfrac{1}{x}\sqrt{1 + \dfrac{-1}{x^2}^2}dx > \int_1^∞2π\dfrac{1}{x}dx = \lim_{t\to ∞}2π(\ln(t)) = ∞
$$

$$
    V = \int_1^∞π\dfrac{1}{x^2}dx = π
$$
