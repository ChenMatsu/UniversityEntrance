### Counting

**Discrete Uniform Law**
- Assume $\Omega$ consists of $n$ equally likely elements
- Assume $A$ consists of $k$ elements

$$
    P(A) = \dfrac{k}{n}
$$

**Permutation**

$$
    P_k^n = \dfrac{n!}{(n-k)!} = n(n - 1)...(n-k + 1)
$$

**Combination**

$$
    C_k^n = \dfrac{n!}{(n - k)!k!}
$$

$$
    \sum_{k=0}^n\begin{pmatrix} n \\ k \end{pmatrix} = 2^n
$$
