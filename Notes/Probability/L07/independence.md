### Independence

$$
    P(A \cap B) = P(A) \cdot P(B)
$$

$$
    P_{X|A}(x) = P_X(x) \text{ for all x}
$$

$$
    P_{X,Y}(x,y) = P_X(x)P_Y(y) = \text{ for all x, y}
$$

$$
    P_{X,Y,Z}(x,y,z) = P_X(x)P_Y(y)P_Z(z)
$$

If $X, Y$ are indepedent:

$$
    E[XY] = E[X]E[Y] \\
    V[X+Y] = V[X]+V[Y]
$$


If $g(X), h(Y)$ are indepedent:

$$
    E[g(X)h(Y)] = E[g(X)]E[h(Y)]
$$
