### Exponential

$$
    \begin{align*}
        f_X(x) = \begin{cases} λ e^{-λ x}, & x \le 0 \\ 0, & x < 0\end{cases}
    \end{align*}
$$

$$
    \begin{align*}
        \int_0^∞λe^{-λx}dx = 1
    \end{align*}
$$

$$
    \begin{align*}
        E[X] = \int_0^∞xλe^{-λx}dx = \dfrac{1}{λ} \\
        E[X^2] = \int_0^∞x^2λe^{-λx}dx = \dfrac{2}{λ^2} \\
        V[X] = E[X^2] - E[X]^2 = \dfrac{1}{λ^2}
    \end{align*}
$$
