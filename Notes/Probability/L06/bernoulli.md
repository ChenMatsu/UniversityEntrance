### Bernoulli

$$
     \begin{align*}
        X = \begin{cases} 1, & p \\ 0, & 1-p \end{cases}
    \end{align*}
$$

$$
    \begin{align*}
        V[X] & = \sum_x(x - E[X])^2P_X(x) \\ & = (1-p)^2p + (0-p)^2*(1-p) \\& = (1-p)[(1-p)p + p^2] = (1-p)p
    \end{align*}
$$

$$
    \begin{align*}
        V[X] & = E[X^2] - (E[X])^2 \\ & = p - p^2 \\ &= p(1-p)
    \end{align*}
$$
