### Conditional PMF and Expectation

$$
    P_{X|A}(x) = P(X = x | A)
$$

$$
    \sum_xP_{X|A}(x) = 1
$$

$$
    E[X|A] = \sum_xxP_{X|A}(x)
$$

$$
    E[g(X)|A] = \sum_xg(x)P_{X|A}(x)
$$

---

**Example**

$$
    E[X] = 2.5 \\
    V[X] = 1.25
$$

$$
    \text{Let } A = {X \ge 2}
$$

$$
    E[X|A] = 3 \\
    V[X|A] = \dfrac{2}{3}
$$
