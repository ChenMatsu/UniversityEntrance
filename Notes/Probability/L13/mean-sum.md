### Sum of a random number of independent r.v

**Example**

- $N$: number of store visited. 
- $X_i$: money spent in store $i$
  - $X_i$ independent, identically distributed
    
$$
    Y = X_1 + ... + X_N \\
    E[Y|N=n] = E[X_1+...+X_N|N=n] = E[X_1+...+X_n|N=n] \\ 
    = E[X_1+...+X_n] = nE[X]
$$
