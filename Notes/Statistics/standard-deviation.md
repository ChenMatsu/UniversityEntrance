#### Standard Deviation

| Concept | Population | Sample |
| :-----| ----: | :----: |
| Mean | $\mu = \dfrac{\sum_{i=1}^Nx_i}{N}$ | $\bar x = \dfrac{\sum_{i=1}^nx_i}{n}$ |
| Variance | $\sigma^2 = \dfrac{\sum_{i=1}^N(x_i - \mu)^2}{N}$ | $s^2 = \dfrac{\sum_{i=1}^n(x_i - \bar x)^2}{n-1}$
| Std | $\sigma$ | $s$
